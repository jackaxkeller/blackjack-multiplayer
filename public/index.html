Arcade Blackjack Royale

A neon-felt blackjack table with animated dealing, casino-style options, generated sound effects, bot opponents, and a speed dial that can turn the table from smoky slow-burn to lightning felt.

Bot Table Setup

1 bot

Bots share the selected skill level. Expert bots follow a strong S17, six-deck, late-surrender basic strategy with doubles and one split. Easy bots are more likely to wander into the velvet fog. The deal-speed slider changes both card animation timing and bot decision tempo, from syrupy suspense to casino pinball.

Table Rules

  • Six-deck shoe, dealer stands on all 17s.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2.
  • Double down allowed on any initial two cards, including after split.
  • Late surrender allowed before you hit, stand, double, or split.
  • Split identical ranks once. Split aces receive one card each and automatically stand.
  • Dealer peeks for blackjack. Pushes return your bet intact.
